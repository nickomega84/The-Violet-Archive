01. Midnight Poems

Glass Hearts

†

The static hums through shattered glass,
A digital pulse in the midnight rain.
We hide our faces in the glowing screen,
Waiting for the signal to start again...

03. Diary of Shadows

October 31st.

†

The outside world is obsessed with the bright,
the corporate, the flawless.
But there is an undeniable beauty in distorted synthesizers,
victorian lace, and the constant autumn rain.
